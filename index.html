Safe Bike Network To Schools


The Safe Bike Network Map Application's primary purpose is to encourage school students to take bicycle as the means of transporation to school, based on the bike network data provided by OpenDataPhilly.

However, recently, due to the frequent incidents in Philadelphia, parents worried about the safety of riding bicycle to school instead of taking shuttle. The map application also provides the incidents information (purple circleMarker) during and around school time.

By dropping a marker on the map (maybe your neighborhood or the safest place you find according to incidents information), you can find the 10 nearest schools (blue circleMarker) and 1 nearest hospital (green circleMarker).
